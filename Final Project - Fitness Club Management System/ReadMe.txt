Main Page - Press the button that best describes your account type - member, trainer or admin.

Members:
They can log in to see a schedule and some session info. Once done, please log out.

Trainers:
They can log in to see a generic gym schedule, overall schedule (every trainers schedule if they have one), and submit attendance for a member. Once done, please log out.

Admin:
Only one admin - created on setup. They can modify a trainers schedule, create a member or trainer, modify a member or trainer, and delete a member or trainer. Once done, please log out.
